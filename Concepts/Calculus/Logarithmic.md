Another perspective on exponents & indices.
### Explanation:
$$
2^5 = 2*2*2*2*2 = 32
$$
What does this really mean?
- Rate
- Time
- Result

Why is this 1?
$$
2^0 = 1 
$$
Raising by -1 results in a fraction.
$$
2^{-1} = \frac{1}{2}
$$

*"Log base 2 of 32 equals 5"*
$$
\log_{2}32 = 5
$$

Logarithmic's are the inverses of exponents.
- Base: rate
- Exponent: result
- Solution: time
$$
\log_{3}81 = 4
$$
